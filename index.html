Our Mission

We carry experimental technology to the finish line.

We are a family of scientists, entrepreneurs and investors dedicated to taking cutting edge technology the final leg. Building upon the legacies of academic, research, and governental lab partners, Cobalt creates value where it matter most, and shares it with the market at large.

Founded in service of the Florida Institute of Human and Machine Cognition, Cobalt combines fundamental understanding of technology, sophisticated investment research, and passionate teams to turn sponsored research into commercial unicorns.
